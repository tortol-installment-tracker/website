Support
Frequently Asked Questions

Does Tortol require an internet connection?

No. Tortol is fully offline. All your data is stored locally on your device and never sent anywhere.

What happens to my data if I reinstall the app?

On Android, reinstalling the app will clear local data (Isar database). Back up your data before uninstalling. Your purchase will be automatically restored via Google Play or the App Store — you won't need to pay again.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Simply download the app and sign in with the same Google or Apple account you used to purchase. The store will automatically restore your purchase at no extra charge.

I forgot my PIN. What can I do?

For security reasons, there is no PIN recovery. If you forget your PIN and exhaust all attempts, the only option is to reinstall the app, which will reset all local data.

Is the ₱149 a subscription?

No. It is a one-time payment. Pay once, use forever — on any device with the same account.

I need help or found a bug. How do I contact you?

Send an email to support.tortol@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
